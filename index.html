About

I'm a genuinely happy person who loves life and you usually see me with a big smile on my face. I have a wonderful life and career. Im laid back, silly and laughter is a must. Albeit, I'm serious when I have to be but I always see the bright side of situations. I've learned that life is too short and you need to live it to its fullest. I work out with a trainer twice a week lifting and cardio. My trainer keeps me in line!! I love being outdoors hiking, boating, sunning my buns or heck, I'll even fish with ya! What I'm NOT looking for is a serial dater. Too many people do not put enough time and effort into dating and getting to know someone or even making relationships work because there is the option of getting back online and finding the next one. If you like what you have read and you're curious to learn more... message me!
